# Bitwise OR of All Even Numbers

## Problem Statement
You are given an integer array `nums`.

Return the **bitwise OR** of all **even numbers** in the array.

If there are **no even numbers**, return **0**.

---

## Example 1
**Input:**  

nums = [1,2,3,4,5,6]

**Output:**  

6

**Explanation:**  
Even numbers → 2, 4, 6  
Bitwise OR → `2 | 4 | 6 = 6`

---

## Example 2
**Input:**  

nums = [7,9,11]

**Output:**  

0

**Explanation:**  
No even numbers → result is `0`.

---

## Example 3
**Input:**  

nums = [1,8,16]

**Output:**  

24

## Constraints

- 1 ≤ nums.length ≤ 100
- 1 ≤ nums[i] ≤ 100
